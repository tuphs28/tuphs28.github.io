Hi! My name is Tom and welcome to my blog! I am a student at the University of Cambridge currently working towards my Master's of Philosophy in Machine Learning and Machine Intelligence who wants to work on ensuring that advanced machine learning does as much good as possible! 

This website is more or less just a personal log that lets me look back at things that I think are interesting or keep record of random projects that I work on. The three broad topics that fascinate me most are the empirical theory of deep learning, ensuring that advanced models are robustly beneficial, and statistical machine learning. However, I expect that this blog will cover a much wider array of random cool topics!

Outside of ML, I love playing guitar (huge fan of the blues!) and hiking / nature (the picture on the left is me at Yosemite - one of the most amazing places I have been lucky enough to visit!). Feel free to get in contact if anything I write about interests you!
